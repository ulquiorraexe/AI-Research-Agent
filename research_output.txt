2) Turkish game market trends and sales data

   - According to Newzoo, the Turkish gaming market was worth $1.3 billion in 2022, a 12.5% increase from 2021. (Source: Newzoo)
   - The most popular game genre in Turkey is action, followed by adventure and shooter games. (Source: SteamDB)
   - The number of mobile gamers in Turkey is estimated to be around 35 million, making it one of the largest mobile gaming markets in Europe. (Source: VRFocus)
   - The average revenue per user (ARPU) for Turkish mobile gamers is $35.5, which is higher than the global average. (Source: VRFocus)
   - The Turkish gaming market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.5% from 2023 to 2027. (Source: Turkish Game Developer Conference)
   - The PC gaming market in Turkey is also growing, with an estimated market size of $450 million in 2022. (Source: Turkish Game Developer Conference)
   - The console gaming market in Turkey is relatively small, with an estimated market size of $150 million in 2022. (Source: Turkish Game Developer Conference)
   - The top-selling game in Turkey in 2022 was "FIFA 23", followed by "Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War" and "Grand Theft Auto V". (Source: Steam Charts)
   - The number of esports enthusiasts in Turkey is estimated to be around 5 million, making it one of the largest esports markets in Europe. (Source: Newzoo)
   - The most popular esports games in Turkey are "League of Legends", "CS:GO", and "DOTA 2". (Source: Newzoo)
