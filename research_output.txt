   - The number of mobile gamers in Turkey is expected to reach 38.6 million in 2021. (Source: Newzoo)
   - The average revenue per user (ARPU) for mobile gamers in Turkey is $21.10. (Source: Newzoo)
   - The most popular game genres in Turkey are action, adventure, and puzzle games. (Source: SteamDB)
   - The most popular platforms for gaming in Turkey are mobile, PC, and console. (Source: Steam Charts)
   - The most popular payment methods for gaming in Turkey are credit/debit cards, mobile payments, and prepaid cards. (Source: Global Game Jam)
