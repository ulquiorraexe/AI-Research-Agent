2) Turkish game market trends and sales data

   - Turkey's gaming market is expected to reach $1.1 billion in 2021, growing 14.5% year-on-year. (Source: Newzoo)
   - Mobile gaming accounts for 48% of the Turkish gaming market, followed by PC gaming at 32% and console gaming at 20%. (Source: GamesIndustry.biz)
   - The number of mobile gamers in Turkey is expected to reach 38.6 million in 2021, with the average revenue per user (ARPU) of $30. (Source: VRFocus)
   - The top-selling Turkish game on Steam in the last 12 months is "Mount & Blade II: Bannerlord" by TaleWorlds Entertainment. (Source: SteamDB)
   - The second best-selling Turkish game on Steam is "ISLANDERS" by Grizzly Games. (Source: Steam Charts)
   - The third best-selling Turkish game on Steam is "Mr. Prepper" by Rejected Games. (Source: Global Game Jam)
