2) Turkish game market trends and sales data

   - The Turkish gaming market was worth $620 million in 2019, a 25% increase from 2018 (Source: Newzoo).
   - Mobile gaming is the largest segment of the Turkish gaming market, accounting for 52% of the market's revenue (Source: Newzoo).
   - The number of mobile gamers in Turkey is expected to reach 35 million by 2022 (Source: Newzoo).
   - The average revenue per user (ARPU) for Turkish mobile gamers is $30.4, the highest in the Middle East and North Africa region (Source: Newzoo).
   - The PC gaming market in Turkey is expected to grow by 10% in 2020 (Source: GamesIndustry.biz).
   - The console gaming market in Turkey is expected to grow by 15% in 2020 (Source: GamesIndustry.biz).
   - The most popular game genres in Turkey are action, adventure, and sports (Source: SteamDB).
   - The most popular game developers in Turkey are TaleWorlds Entertainment, MadGames Tycoon, and Ozdil Aydogan (Source: SteamDB).
   - The most popular game publishers in Turkey are Paradox Interactive, Daedalic Entertainment, and Assemble Entertainment (Source: SteamDB).
   - The most popular game engines in Turkey are Unity, Unreal Engine, and Godot (Source: Game Developer).
