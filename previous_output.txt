   - The PC gaming market in Turkey is expected to generate $350 million in revenue in 2020, growing 10.3% year-on-year. (Source: Newzoo)
   
   - The console gaming market in Turkey is expected to generate $150 million in revenue in 2020, growing 12.5% year-on-year. (Source: Newzoo)
   
   - The average revenue per user (ARPU) for mobile gamers in Turkey is $16.01, compared to $105.25 for PC gamers and $130.51 for console gamers. (Source: Newzoo)
   
   - The most popular game genres in Turkey are action, adventure, and puzzle games. (Source: SteamDB)
   
   - The most popular platforms for gaming in Turkey are mobile, PC, and console. (Source: Newzoo)
   
   - The most popular payment methods for gaming in Turkey are credit/debit cards, mobile payments, and prepaid cards. (Source: Newzoo)
   
   - The most popular age group for gaming in Turkey is 18-34 years old. (Source: Newzoo)
