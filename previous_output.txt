2) Turkish game market trends and sales data

   - Turkey ranks 17th in the global games market with a revenue of $1.6 billion in 2020, a 19.2% year-on-year growth (Source: Newzoo).
   - Mobile games generated 53% of the total gaming revenue in Turkey in 2020 (Source: Newzoo).
   - The number of mobile gamers in Turkey reached 35.2 million in 2020, with a year-on-year growth of 11.3% (Source: Newzoo).
   - The average revenue per paying user (ARPPU) in Turkey is $42.5, which is lower than the global average of $54.2 (Source: Newzoo).
   - The most popular game genres in Turkey are action, adventure, and simulation (Source: Istanbul Game Festival).
   - The Turkish game development industry has grown by 35% in 2020, with a total revenue of ₺1.2 billion (~$200 million) (Source: TGDA).
