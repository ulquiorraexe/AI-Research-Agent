2) Turkish game market trends and sales data

   - Turkey's games market is expected to generate $820 million in revenue in 2020, a 10.4% increase from 2019 (Source: Newzoo).
   - Mobile games are the largest segment in Turkey's games market, accounting for 55% of the total revenue (Source: Newzoo).
   - The number of gamers in Turkey is projected to reach 35.2 million in 2020 (Source: Newzoo).
   - The PC games market in Turkey is expected to generate $205 million in revenue in 2020 (Source: Newzoo).
   - The console games market in Turkey is expected to generate $115 million in revenue in 2020 (Source: Newzoo).
   - The average revenue per user (ARPU) in Turkey's games market is projected to reach $23.3 in 2020 (Source: Newzoo).
   - The most popular game genres in Turkey are action, shooter, and sports (Source: GamesIndustry.biz).
   - The most popular platforms in Turkey are mobile, PC, and console (Source: GamesIndustry.biz).
   - The Turkish games market is expected to reach $1.1 billion in revenue by 2023 (Source: GamesIndustry.biz).
   - The Turkish games market is the 19th largest games market in the world (Source: GamesIndustry.biz).
